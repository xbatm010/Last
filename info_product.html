DESCRIPTION

The Blond AD-42S NA acoustic guitar is an affordable instrument suitable for both beginners and more experienced players. This is a classic Dreadnought with a solid spruce top, laminated rosewood back and sides, okoume neck, modern composite fingerboard, closed die-cast tuners and a gloss finish front. The Blond AD-42S NA acoustic guitar combines affordability with quality craftsmanship, making it an excellent choice for musicians at any skill level. Whether you're just starting your musical journey or looking for a reliable instrument to add to your collection, this guitar delivers a classic Dreadnought design with a host of features that enhance both playability and tone.
